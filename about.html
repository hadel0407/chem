About This Site

This personal chemical engineering website focuses on applying fundamental theory to solve real-world production problems.

Our mission is to help chemical and process engineers understand how thermodynamics, phase equilibrium, and mass transfer principles can be practically applied in industrial systems.

Articles emphasize practical assumptions, boundary conditions, and limitations of commonly used formulas, particularly under non-ideal and real operating conditions.

Content is designed for engineers, students, and professionals seeking to bridge theory and practice, improve efficiency, troubleshoot processes, and optimize chemical production safely.

All material is provided for educational and professional knowledge exchange.
